    Our Story

    Founded by passionate coffee enthusiasts from Yemen, Mocha Café brings a unique blend of tradition and modernity to Kigali. Our mission is to provide a cozy, friendly environment where everyone feels welcome.

    Mocha Cafe not only a cafe

    Welcome to Mocha Cafe, a charming eatery located in Gacuriro. Our cafe offers a unique fusion of Yemeni, Arabic, and International cuisine, providing a diverse and delightful dining experience. Whether you're craving traditional Yemeni Food, a hearty American breakfast, or an authentic Italian espresso, Mocha Cafe is your go-to destination for delicious meals and a welcoming atmosphere. Join us for an unforgettable culinary journey!

    Shakshuka

    Indulge in our savory Shakshuka, a vibrant dish featuring perfectly fried eggs nestled in a rich, spiced tomato and onions. Garnished with fresh herbs and served with pita bread, this Middle Eastern classic is a comforting and flavorful experience that's perfect for any time of the day.

    Lebnah

    Experience the creamy delight of our traditional Lebnah, a rich and tangy strained yogurt. Drizzled with olive oil, this Middle Eastern delicacy is perfect as a spread, dip, or accompaniment. Enjoy it with warm pita bread or fresh vegetables for a refreshing and wholesome treat.

    Zurbian

    Indulge in the rich and aromatic flavors of our traditional Zurbian. This Yemeni delicacy features tender pieces of marinated beef or chicken, perfectly cooked with fragrant basmati rice, potatoes, and a blend of exotic spices. Garnished with crispy fried onions, Zurbian offers a delightful and satisfying culinary experience that transports you to the heart of Yemen with every bite.

    Falafel

    Enjoy our delicious Falafel, a Middle Eastern favorite made from a blend of ground chickpeas, fresh herbs, and aromatic spices. These crispy, golden-brown patties are perfect in a warm pita or flatbread, comes with tomto souce and creamy hummus. Each bite offers a delightful combination of textures and flavors, making Falafel a satisfying and wholesome meal or snack.
